ABOUT

IIM Ahmedabad, founded in 1961 is the second oldest IIM and is consistently ranked as the best business school of India. IIM Ahmedabad has been ranked 1 by NIRF 2023, among the top 100 Indian B-Schools. IIM Ahmedabad is known for its top-ranked 2 year PGP (MBA) program which offers admissions through CAT. IIM Ahmedabad cutoff remains around 80 percentile in CAT for shortlisting and students with over 99 percentile have a good chance of getting the interview call. IIM Ahmedabad is known for its stellar placements in the Consulting and Finance domain.
